1. Introduction

Deposit Defender ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application ("App"). By using the App, you consent to the data practices described in this Privacy Policy.

2.1 Personal Information You Provide

We collect information you directly provide to us, including:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, phone number
  • Profile Information: User preferences, settings, profile picture
  • Communication Data: Messages, support requests, feedback
  • Document Information: Rental agreements, receipts, and other documents you choose to store
  • Payment Information: Billing details processed through Apple's App Store (we do not directly store payment card information)

2.2 Information Automatically Collected

When you use our App, we automatically collect certain information:

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, unique device identifiers
  • Usage Data: App usage patterns, features accessed, time spent in app
  • Log Data: IP address, access times, app crashes, performance data
  • Location Data: General geographic location (with your permission)

2.3 Information from Third Parties

We may collect information from:

  • Apple App Store: Purchase history, subscription status
  • Social Media: If you connect social media accounts (with your permission)
  • Analytics Providers: Aggregated usage statistics and performance metrics
